About Us

AgriOptix was founded to bring modern computer vision to everyday farm workflows. We’re developing AI-powered systems that automate and enhance daily agricultural operations — starting with bale counting and weighbridge automation.

Bale Counting

Calibrate once, then count rows × columns from a rectified trailer face. Lightweight, fast, and accurate in real-world conditions.

Weighbridge Automation

Edge Jetson devices detect trailers, capture snapshots, and post load summaries to a central dashboard with operator overrides.
